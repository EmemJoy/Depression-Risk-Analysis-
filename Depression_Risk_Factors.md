# Depression-Risk-Analysis-
An interactive Power BI dashboard that analyzes depression risk by examining demographic trends, mental health indicators, and health related factors, enabling data-driven insights for healthcare decision making.

# Project Overview
Mental health has become an increasingly important public health concern worldwide. Understanding the factors associated with depression can help healthcare professionals, policymakers, and researchers identify high-risk populations and support early intervention strategies.
This project explores depression-related data using Power BI to uncover patterns and trends across demographic and lifestyle variables.

# Business Problem
Healthcare organizations need reliable insights to identify populations at higher risk of depression and understand the factors contributing to mental health outcomes.
This dashboard helps answer questions such as:
- Which demographic groups have the highest depression risk?
- How do lifestyle factors influence depression?
- Which age groups are most affected?
- Are there differences in depression risk by gender?
- Which factors appear to have the strongest relationship with depression?

# Objectives
- Clean and prepare mental health data
- Analyze depression risk across demographic groups
- Identify trends and patterns in mental health indicators
- Build an interactive Power BI dashboard
- Support evidence-based healthcare decision-making
